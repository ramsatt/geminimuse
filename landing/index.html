What AI tools work with Gemini Muse prompts? +

All prompts work with Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly, Google Gemini, and any other text-to-image AI model.

Yes, Gemini Muse is completely free. You get 3 free copies per session on the app, and can unlock more by watching a short ad.

Can I use these prompts for commercial projects? +

Yes. The prompts themselves are free to use for personal and commercial projects. Images generated using the prompts are subject to the terms of the AI platform you use.

Do you add new prompts? +

Yes! We add new prompts every week. The "New This Week" section in the app always shows the latest additions.
